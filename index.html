Our Mission

A Profession Built on Discipline, Independence and Integrity

The ISEA exists to uphold a standard of analytical judgement, ethical conduct and professional discipline that commands respect across jurisdictions. We serve as a reference point for practitioners who recognise that equity analysis is a fiduciary function influencing capital allocation, market integrity and long-term economic outcomes.

Ethical Foundation

Code of Ethics & Professional Conduct

The Society formally adopts the CFA Institute's Code of Ethics and Standards of Professional Conduct, the Research Objectivity Standards and the Best Practice Guidelines Governing Analyst/Corporate Issuer Relations.

Members are required to adhere to principles that demand integrity, independence of judgement, transparency in communication, proper handling of material non-public information, and avoidance of conflicts that could compromise analytical reliability.
