Career & Talent Centre

Where Credentials Carry Practical Weight

A members-only job board, career pathways, compensation benchmarks and employer partnerships — practical support for career progression.

Opportunities

Global Equity Analyst Job Board

The Society's Job Board connects employers with analysts whose credentials and conduct have been vetted against a recognised professional standard. Access is restricted to institutions that engage in genuine equity research, asset management, corporate finance or related analytical functions.

Listings span buy-side and sell-side research positions, portfolio management, corporate access, investor relations and regulatory analysis. Members carry the context of examination performance, CPD compliance and, where applicable, Fellowship review — information that shortens the distance between application and serious consideration.

Career Guidance

Career Pathways & Compensation Benchmarks

The Society provides structured information on how careers in equity analysis typically develop — from entry-level research support through to senior analyst, portfolio management, or leadership positions. Compensation benchmarking data is drawn from confidential member surveys across regions, sectors and seniority levels.

The benchmarking accounts for meaningful variations — sell-side research carries different structures to buy-side portfolio-facing roles, and both differ from independent research houses. Career pathway guidance also addresses the relationship between credentialing and advancement.

Institutional Partnerships

Employer Partner Program

The Employer Partner Program formalises the relationship between the Society and institutions that rely on equity analysis talent. Partnership carries reciprocal obligations — employers support CPD for credentialed employees, provide input into curriculum relevance, and uphold internal research governance consistent with the Society's standards.

In return, Employer Partners receive preferential access to the Job Board, participation in campus recruitment initiatives, and invitations to contribute to discussions on curriculum development and emerging analytical requirements.

Career Support

Resume Review & Executive Coaching

Resume Review is conducted by senior practitioners who understand what distinguishes a substantive research contribution from a procedural one. Members receive direct, candid feedback — an assessment of whether the document accurately reflects the level of judgement the candidate has exercised.

Executive Coaching extends support to senior transitions: movement into portfolio management, research function leadership, or Fellowship-level recognition. Coaching engagements are structured around the specific demands of these transitions, including how to present a track record of independent judgement and navigate conflicts inherent to senior analytical roles.

Access & Opportunity

Internship & Fellowship Grants

The Internship & Fellowship Grants program provides funding to support internship placements and research fellowships for candidates who demonstrate strong analytical potential but face financial constraints. Recipients are selected through a competitive process assessing academic performance, analytical aptitude and understanding of the profession's ethical responsibilities.

The Society's view is that access to the profession should be determined by aptitude and integrity, not solely by financial circumstance — while ensuring those admitted through this pathway are held to the same standard as every other member.
